About Us

YourSitter is an online platform that brings together parents or elderly people in need and matches them with the best possible, trusted sitter satisfying their requirements. YourSitter provides a faster way to book a sitter and at the convenience of your doorstep.

Our website allows everyone: parents, elderly and the sitters to browse, request, post, update availability, list services and book an appointment. YourSitter is driven to deliver the best to its users, buid trust and deliver the best quality service to its customers.
